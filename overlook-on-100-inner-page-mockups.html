Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Whether you're preparing to schedule a tour, submit an application, or move into your new home, we're here to make the process as simple as possible. If you don't see your question here, our leasing team is happy to help.

Leasing & Applications

How do I apply for an apartment?+

Applications can be completed online through our secure resident portal. Once you've found the floor plan that's right for you, simply check availability and begin the application process.

Is there an application fee?+

Yes. A non-refundable application fee of $55 per applicant is required when submitting an application.

Is there an administrative fee?+

Yes. A $150 administrative fee applies to new leases.

What is required to complete my application?+

Applicants may be required to provide identification, income verification, rental history information, and any additional documentation requested during the screening process.

How long does the approval process take?+

Application review times may vary, but most applications are processed as quickly as possible once all required documentation has been received.

Deposits & Leasing Terms

What is the security deposit?+

Security deposits typically range from $500 up to one month's rent, depending on application screening results and other qualifying factors.

What lease terms are available?+

Current lease term options may vary based on availability. Please contact the leasing office for the most up-to-date leasing options.

Do you offer move-in specials?+

Qualified new residents may be eligible for a $500 move-in special. Contact the leasing office for current details, restrictions, and availability.

Pets

Is Overlook on 100 pet friendly?+

Yes. We welcome pets and understand they're an important part of your household.

How many pets are allowed?+

A maximum of two pets per apartment home is permitted.

Are there weight restrictions?+

Yes. Pets must weigh 60 pounds or less.

Are there pet fees?+

A $200 pet fee applies at move-in, along with $25 per month per pet in pet rent.

Are breed restrictions in place?+

Additional pet guidelines or restrictions may apply. Please contact the leasing office for complete details.

Apartment Homes & Amenities

What amenities are available?+

Residents enjoy access to a variety of community amenities, including an outdoor swimming pool, package receiving area, laundry facilities, on-site maintenance, professional management, and guest parking. Apartment homes may also include stainless steel appliances, luxury vinyl plank flooring, balconies, walk-in closets, and updated finishes.

Do all apartments have balconies?+

Balconies are available in select apartment homes. Please review individual floor plan details for availability.

Do all apartments include walk-in closets?+

Walk-in closets are available in select floor plans.

Is parking available?+

Yes. Parking options are available for residents and guests. Contact the leasing office for additional details.

Maintenance & Resident Services

How do I submit a maintenance request?+

Residents can submit maintenance requests through the resident portal at any time.

Is emergency maintenance available?+

Yes. Emergency maintenance support is available for urgent maintenance issues that require immediate attention.

How do I access the resident portal?+

Residents will receive portal access information after move-in. The portal can be used to pay rent, submit maintenance requests, and manage account information.

Scheduling a Tour

How do I schedule a tour?+

You can schedule a tour online or contact our leasing team directly. We'll be happy to show you available apartment homes and answer any questions you may have.

Can I view available apartments before touring?+

Yes. Current pricing and availability can be viewed online through our availability page.

Still Have Questions?

Contact Our Leasing Team

If you need additional information about floor plans, availability, leasing requirements, or community amenities, please don't hesitate to reach out.
